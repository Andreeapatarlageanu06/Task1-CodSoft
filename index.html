Why StudyX?

StudyX was created by students for students. The beginning questions are made to understand your learning schedule better, in order to adapt the study session for you.

  • Easy To Use

    Just press the "Start" button and the app will help you be productive!

  • Grow your productivity

    StudyX will put the background music suited for your productivity and will also announce you when to get a break.

  • Objectives

    StudyX will track if you reached all your objectives and you will get a notification in case you forgot.

  • Data Privacy

    Don't stress over your privacy! Your data is safe with us.
